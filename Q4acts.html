Battle of the Bands
For A.P month, the A.P club conducted a contest wherein
a class representative will be chosen to represent as someone
who is a famous American icon that had shown value. Our
historical icon is Rosa Parks, who is an activist in the civil rights. Together
the class created an outfit for their representative.

One Act Play
For English month, every student is highly encouraged to dress up as a
book character. From Little Red Riding Hood to Manga characters. Almost
every student in the school was able to dress up as one!
Everyone showed off their favorite character through cosplaying.

Zumba
In the 4th to 5th week of November, 9-Family practiced the
choreography for Values Pop, a competition for ESP or Values month
With a theme of youth for change. The performance will be held
on December 16, 2024.

Filipino Monologue
In the 2nd Quarter of P.E, we had to dance cha-cha with our randomly
chosen partners and create our own dance routine based off the steps
that Sir Santos had taught us. This picture was taken after the
MAPEH cha-cha.

Teacher's Day 2024
Before I could forget... on October 4, 2024, LPSci celebrated
World Teacher's Day right when 2nd Quarter just started. Every classroom
had their own celebration in their classrooms. And on this day, there
were "mini-teachers" who pretended who be a teacher for a day and
taught the class instead of the teacher themself.
